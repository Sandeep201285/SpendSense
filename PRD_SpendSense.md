Product Requirements Document: SpendSense (v1.0)1. 
Executive SummarySpendSense is an AI-driven, mobile-first expense intelligence platform designed to automate the tedious process of manual expense logging. By leveraging SMS and Email parsing, the app provides real-time visibility into spending habits, identifies behavioral patterns, and gamifies financial health through a proprietary "Spending Score.

Functional Requirements4.1 Data Integration & ParsingSMS Engine (Android Focus): Permission-based listener to parse incoming bank alerts, UPI notifications, and OTP-based transaction confirmations.Email Engine (Gmail API): OAuth2 integration to scan for digital receipts from major vendors (Amazon, Zomato, etc.).Data Extraction: Use NLP to identify:Merchant: (e.g., "Swiggy", "Uber")Amount: Normalized currency values.Date/Time: For temporal analysis.4.2 Smart CategorizationAuto-Tagging: Rule-based and ML-based engine to bucket transactions into Food, E-commerce, Travel, Bills, Entertainment.User Correction: Allow users to manually re-categorize; the AI must learn from these corrections to improve future accuracy.4.3 AI Behavioral InsightsPattern Recognition: Identify "Impulse Buying" (high-frequency, low-value shopping) and "Temporal Trends" (weekend spikes).Spending Score: A gamified 300–900 metric calculated based on budget adherence, savings rate, and impulse control.4.4 Budgeting & AlertsPredictive Analysis: Algorithm to forecast end-of-month totals based on current velocity.Threshold Alerts: Push notifications at 50%, 80%, and 100% of category limits.5. Technical ArchitectureFrontendFramework: React Native (Cross-platform support with deep Android integration for SMS).State Management: Redux Toolkit or Zustand for handling complex transactional states.Visuals: D3.js or Victory Native for high-fidelity interactive charts.Backend & AI LayerRuntime: Node.js (Express) hosted on scalable cloud infrastructure.Database: PostgreSQL (for structured transactional data) + Redis (for caching insights).AI Engine:Extraction: LLM APIs (GPT-4o or Gemini Pro) for unstructured email/SMS parsing.Classification: Custom Python-based microservice for lightweight categorization.6. Privacy & SecurityEnd-to-End Encryption: AES-256 for data at rest; TLS 1.3 for data in transit.Local Processing: Move as much parsing logic as possible to the device (Edge AI) to minimize sensitive data transmission.Zero Sharing: Strict policy against selling anonymized or identifiable data to third-party advertisers.7.
